Our Mission

At H&H (Homestays for Homeless), we’re committed to ensuring every student finds a safe, affordable, and welcoming place to call home. Founded in 2024 by Apoorv Arora, our platform connects students with verified homestays in Pune, offering transparent details like pricing, location, and amenities—no sign-ups required. We also empower homestay owners to reach more students by listing their properties effortlessly.
